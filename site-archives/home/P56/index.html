Notes from 04.13.11

Jacob wrestled with an angel all evening and at daybreak let him go after his hip was wrenched.  For struggling and overcoming both God and man, he was blessed and given the name ‘Israel’.

I had my pants pulled down in second grade by some cruel classmates who wanted to embarrass me during a rainy day assembly.  Because the entire school saw, I was given the name ‘Little Willy’.

Sometimes life just isn’t fair.
