I would argue that Jews kiss things more than any other religion on the planet.While not a direct mandate, kissing is a way of showing reverence and we do it to all sort of things.

Prayer books.
Mezuzahs.
Tallitot.
Tzit tzit.
Tefillin.
Torah mantles.
Torah scrolls.
Paroches.

Also if you go to Jewish summer camp there’s a fair amount of it going on.
Oh, and don’t forget your mother.
