My Mother recalls the following:

She was going out for an evening of theatre and had dressed accordingly.
A fancy gown, makeup, perfume and her naturally curly hair coiffed perfectly atop her head.
A vision to behold.
Upon entering the theatre a woman stopped her to remark on her appearance.
‘Excuse me’ she exclaimed ‘But where did you get that absolutely stunning perm?!’
My Mother, both surprised and flattered to receive such a compliment in regard to her God-given locks coyly replied ‘Mother Nature’.
Without hesitation the woman responded ‘Oh, where are they located?’
