The Bark Mitzvah, first introduced in 1977, combines the age old Jewish tradition of B’nai mitzvah with the age old Jewish tradition of making awful puns.

At the age of 13 months, owners throw a party for their pet and dress them in a yarmulke and tallis for the occasion.

While some see it as a desecration of sacred practices, others see it as a way to mark their dog’s transition into adulthood.

Regardless, the owner is still required to pick up their excrement.
