After years of watching, leading and participating in horahs I have come up with the following formula to calculate the potential risks of such an endeavor:

(Flailing Of Person On Chair Due To Nervousness+Chiropractic Issues of People Holding The Chair+Inebriation Of Guests)÷(Height Of Ceiling+Structural Integrity of Chair+Upper Body Strength And Endurance)=Probability of Injury due to falling off chair and/or hitting ones head.

All things considered, its amazing that most simchas don’t end with a trip to the E.R.
